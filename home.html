Coir is a great choice for growing indoor plants because it holds water well, retains air, helps control humidity, and eliminates the need for soil or other growing mediums. It also makes a great substrate for planting seedlings that are sensitive to transplanting into the garden.

At CoirCrop, we think all-natural, eco-friendly products are the best way to go. That's why we've created a line of 100% natural coco peats, which come in a variety of sizes and shapes. Our coco peats are made with 100% pure organic coconut shells, so they're perfect for any indoor or outdoor plant—even those that need a little help fighting off pests!
