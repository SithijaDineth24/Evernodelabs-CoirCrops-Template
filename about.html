About Us

Our Story

Coir Crops is a Sri Lankan company with a mission to provide quality 100% natural coir products to customers. We believe in providing the highest quality and value for our customers, which is why we've worked hard over the past few years to develop our business model, including a strong commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing practices.

We only sell products made from natural materials and we always try to source the best possible ingredients for our products through fair trade agreements with local farmers and cooperatives who work directly with their communities.
We're proud of what we've built here in Sri Lanka, but we know that there's more room for improvement—and you can help us make it happen!

Who We Are

Coir Products Company is a Sri Lankan company that specializes in the production of 100% natural coir products. We aim to provide our customers with quality products at affordable prices and strive to meet every customer's specific needs in their area of interest.

We are located in Dabana, Sri Lanka. Our main office is located on Somapala Road, Jambugas handiya, Dabana.
We have been in business since 2013 and have grown to become one of the largest suppliers of coir products in the country today. We are working around the clock so that we can ensure the best quality products and services for our customers.
